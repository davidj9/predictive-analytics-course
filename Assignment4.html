  1. Describe the relationship between coffee consumption and blood pressure and explain what someone should do if their doctor says that their blood pressure is a concern.

  • The graph shows a positive correlation between blood pressure and number of cups of coffee consumed. The confidence that can be placed in this relationship will be assessed as part of the linear regression model created in step 2.

  • Perhaps more significantly many of the participants have elevated blood pressure. Many of the observations are in hypertension stage 1 (130 - 139) or stage 2 (140 or higher). (Source: Healthy and Unhealthy Blood Pressure Ranges)

  • Reducing the coffee they consume seems prudent under these circumstances, but they should also consult with their doctor if other interventions are advised.

  1. Train the model and predict the values for 8 and 6 cups of coffee in a day.

  • This will be accomplished by creating a linear regression model between blood pressure and number of cups of coffee for the observations and drawing conclusions on the robustness of the model and its predictions.

  • Initialize random number generator for reproducibility

    set.seed(1)
